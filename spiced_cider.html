Spiced Cider

Ingredients Amounts
Cloves .5 teaspoon
Star Anise 1
Cinnamon Sticks 2
Apple Cider 1 cup
Rum 1oz
Orange Slice 1
Apple Slice 1

Instructions

  1. Made hot chocolate
  2. Toast spices in a pot over medium heat for 2-3 minutes
  3. Reduce heat and add cider to simmer for 30 minutes
  4. Turn off heat and add Rum
  5. Serve hot, or wait to cool and serve cold
  6. Garnish with cinnamon stick and apple slice
  7. Enjoy :)
